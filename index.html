Chika Maduabuchi

I am a graduate Machine Learning researcher at the MIT Red Lab, working under the supervision of Professors Matteo Bucci and Ericmoore Jossou. My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision and large vision-language models towards developing generalizable multimodal AI for social good.

Before joining MIT, I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree at the University of Nigeria Nsukka fortunate to be supervised by Professors Chigbo Mgbemene, Howard Njoku, and Chigbogu Ozoegwu. After that, I founded the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory where I lead research on developing AI models for social good.
